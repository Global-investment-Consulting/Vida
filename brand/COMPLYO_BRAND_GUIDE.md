# Complyo Brand Guide

## Core Identity
- **Name:** Complyo
- **Tagline:** "Compliance that runs itself."
- **Mission:** Automate European compliance and invoicing through intelligent APIs.
- **Voice:** Trustworthy, clear, optimistic. Write for humans, not bureaucrats.

## Logo
- **Wordmark:** lowercase complyo
- **Typeface:** Inter or Satoshi Bold
- **Symbol:** Two interlocking circles representing the compliance loop
- **Safe-space ratio:** Maintain 1Ã— padding around the wordmark on all sides
- **Favicon:** Green circle with white `c`

## Typography
- **Primary Typeface:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Optional Headline Typeface:** Satoshi or DM Sans
- **Recommended Font Weights:** 400, 600, 700

## UI Components
- **Buttons:** `rounded-xl`; primary uses Complyo blue with white text; secondary uses neutral gray.
- **Cards:** Apply `shadow-sm` with `border-slate-200` for separation.
- **Navigation:** Left sidebar highlighting the active item with the accent color.
- **Dark Mode:** Invert neutral tones; shift primary blue to `#3B82F6`.

## Iconography
- **Icon Set:** Lucide or Heroicons (outline styles)
- **Stroke Width:** Consistent 1.5px line weight

## Tone
- Position Complyo as an independent tech brand aiming for confidence and clarity.
- Do not reference or visually connect to E.ME.KO or Argentor.
